About FeedbackMgmt

FeedbackMgmt is a lightweight feedback management system built to help educational institutions collect meaningful feedback from students and act on it quickly.

Our goal is to make feedback collection simple, secure, and insightful. The platform supports student, course and faculty management, customizable questions and easy-to-read feedback summaries.
